About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. Jiashi Feng. Previously, I have spent some fun time doing research at CUHKSZ-Tencent AI Lab Joint Laboratory, Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab (AI Theory Group) and National University of Singapore (NExT++ Lab).

My research lies in the theory and application of Machine Learning, specifically in the following topics:

  • Algorithms and new model architectures for federated learning.
  • Model inversion and gradient inversion, especially in data-free knowledge reconstruction from pre-trained models and zero-shot knowledge transfer.
  • Personalization and causal inference in recommender system.
