About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. Jiashi Feng. Previously, I have spent some fun time doing research at CUHKSZ-Tencent AI Lab Joint Laboratory, Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab (AI Theory Group), National University of Singapore (NExT++ Lab), and Southeast University and also have been an algorithm intern at the NLP group of Huawei Nanjing Research Institute.

My research lies in the theory and application of Machine Learning, specifically in Recommender Systems and Federated Learning. I am most excited about creating intelligent systems that can analyze human emotion, mimic the human learning process and figure out the essence of human logic.
