About Me

I am a Research Assistant at CUHKSZ-Tencent AI Lab Joint Laboratory, supervised by Prof. Tong Zhang. Previously, I have spent some fun time doing research at Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab (AI Theory Group), National University of Singapore (NExT++ Lab), and Southeast University and also have been an algorithm intern at the NLP group of Huawei Nanjing Research Institute.

My research lies in the theory and application of Machine Learning, specifically in Recommender Systems, Natural Language Processing, and Social Media Analysis. I am most excited about creating intelligent systems that can analyze human emotion, mimic the human learning process and figure out the essence of human logic.

Research Experiences

  • Oct 2019 - Present

    Neural Architecture Search with Meta-Learning
    CUHKSZ-Tencent AI Lab Joint Laboratory, Advisor: Prof. Tong Zhang.
    - Aim to improve current Neural Architecture Search (NAS) methods with a focus on making the searched neural architectures more adaptive and transferable. We formulated this problem under the setting of few-shot learning and introduced a meta-learner to guide the architecture search task.

  • Jul 2019 - Oct 2019

    Meta-Learning for Model Selection in Recommender System
    Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab, Advisor: Zhenguo Li, Fei Chen, Zhenhua Dong.
    - We proposed a meta-learning-based framework called MetaSelector to blend the strengths of multiple recommender, conduct experiments on Movielens-1M, Amazon, and Production data and demonstrate that our proposed framework achieves significant improvements over single recommender baselines in terms of AUC and Logloss.

  • Jan 2019 - May 2019

    Federated Meta-Learning
    Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab, Advisor: Zhenguo Li, Fei Chen, Zhenhua Dong.
    - Improve federated learning algorithms by introducing the idea of meta-learning, finish the code for FedMeta framework and conduct experiments on LEAF which is a benchmark for Federated Learning.

  • Sep 2018 - Nov 2018

    Knowledge Extraction from Large-scale Unstructured Texts
    Huawei Nanjing Research Institute, Natural Language Processing Intern.
    - Construct knowledge graph based on large-scale unstructured texts crawled from Wikipedia and Baidu Baike. Conduct word segmentation, entity relationship recognition based on statistical and deep learning methods.

  • Jul 2018 - Sep 2018

    Recommendation with Knowledge-aware Negative Sampler
    National University of Singapore (NExT++ Lab), Advisor: Prof. Tat-Seng Chua and Dr. Xiang Wang.
    - Introduce adversarial learning framework to improve the performances of a wide range of existing knowledge graph embedding models. Use the negative samples generated by knowledge graph embedding model to improving the performance of collaborative filtering discriminator.

  • May 2017 - May 2018

    Data Mining and Visualization Analysis of Social Networks
    China Network Valley, Advisor: Prof. Zhongyuan Qin.
